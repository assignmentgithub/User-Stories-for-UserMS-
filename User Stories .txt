User Stories 

UserMS:

User Registration and Authentication:

* As a user, I want to register for an account to access the shopping platform
* As a user, I want to log in securely to access my account and personalized features

Profile Management:

* As a user, I want to update my profile information (name, address, contact details) easily
* As a user, I want to manage my preferences, such as notification settings or subscription choiceS

Security and Access Control:

* As a user, I want my personal information and account details to be secured and protected
* As a user, I want to recover/reset my password securely in case I forget it

Order and Transaction History:

* As a user, I want to view my order history to track past purchases and their statuses
* As a user, I want access to transaction details and invoices for my orders

Address and Payment Management:

* As a user, I want to manage multiple shipping addresses for convenient delivery options
* As a user, I want to securely manage and update my payment methods

Account Management and Security:

* As a user, I want to easily modify or delete my account if needed
* As a user, I want to log out securely to protect my account from unauthorized access

Communication Preferences:

* As a user, I want to control the frequency and type of notifications or communications I receive
